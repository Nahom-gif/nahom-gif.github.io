Social Media Manager

At Republic, I was their first social media manager onboarded to the brand. I served as a visionary for social media and digital content strategy, acting as a proactive subject matter expert in social and digital media. I developed and executed social media strategy for an audience of 150k+ across Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Linkedin, Discord, and Telegram to increase engagement and reach by 10% month over month. I collaborated with our Head of Brand to develop our brand voice and create compelling messaging tailored to each social channel.

Enhancing Brand Growth and Engagement at Republic

Challenges

  • Republic sought to increase its brand awareness and engagement within the competitive crowdfunding market, targeting a diverse audience of potential investors and project creators. The primary challenge was to stand out in a saturated market and increase user participation and investment in campaigns.

Solution

    Comprehensive Social Media Strategy:

    • Audience Analysis: Conducted a deep dive into audience demographics, and behavior using tools like Buffer and Google Analytics.
    • Content Diversification: Developed a content calendar that balanced educational, promotional, and community-building posts tailored to the audience's interests and investment trends.
    • Platform Optimization: Utilized different strategies for various platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, Instagram) to maximize reach and engagement.

      Data-Driven Approach:

    • Engagement Metrics: Implemented a metrics-driven strategy to track engagement, reach, and conversion, refining content types and posting schedules based on data insights.
    • A/B Testing: Conducted A/B tests for different content formats and messaging to determine what resonated best with the audience.

      • Collaborative Campaigns:

    • Cross-Departmental Alignment: Worked closely with the Head of Brand and other departments to align the social media strategy with ongoing campaigns and company values.
    • Investor Stories: Showcased success stories from investors and businesses who benefited from Republic, creating a narrative of success and trust.
